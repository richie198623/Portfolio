Forza Horizon 2 - Fast and Furious - Playground Games / Turn 10 Studios / XBOX ONE

This project was created to coincide with the release of Fast and the Furious 7. I was tasked with combining the themes of Fast and Furious into the world of Horizon. This saw the introduction of a NOS meter to the speedometer, a reskin to the UI menu's and colour grading the in-game world in order to be consistent with the films aesthetic.
